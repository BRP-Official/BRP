Warning System

W1 — Warning
W2 — Last Chance
W3 — Kick
W4 — Temporary Ban
W7 — Permanent Ban

Staff will use these codes to indicate severity when enforcing rules.

1. RDM — Random Deathmatch

Killing or attempting to kill players without a valid roleplay reason is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning for first offence; repeated offences may lead to temporary ban.

2. Attacking Staff

Attacking, killing, or shooting staff members is strictly prohibited.

Consequence: Immediate temporary ban or multi-day ban depending on severity.

3. NLR — New Life Rule

After respawning, you must roleplay as a new character and may not return to your previous life or continue the same scenario.

Consequence: Warning; repeat offences may lead to temporary ban.

4. VDM — Vehicle Deathmatch

Damaging or destroying another player's vehicle outside of proper roleplay or outside of designated areas is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning for minor incidents; repeated or severe incidents may lead to temporary ban.

5. Cuff Rushing

Unlawfully cuffing or restraining a player without proper roleplay or without announcing the action (e.g., "~cuffs") is not allowed.

Consequence: Warning; repeated offences may lead to temporary ban.

6. Auto-Jailing

Automatically jailing a criminal without transporting them to the appropriate facility or following proper procedure is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning; repeated offences may lead to stricter punishments.

7. Disrespecting Staff

Disrespecting staff members or failing to follow their instructions is not tolerated.

Consequence: Warning; severe or repeated disrespect may lead to temporary or permanent bans.

8. Leaving to Avoid Punishment (LTAP)

Leaving the game to avoid a kick, ban, or other punishment is not allowed. Staff can issue punishments even if you are not in the game.

Consequence: Immediate ban in severe cases; temporary ban for lesser offences.

9. FRP — Failing Roleplay

Failing to roleplay, breaking character, or refusing to participate reasonably in a roleplay scenario is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning; persistent failing roleplay may lead to temporary ban.

10. Fear Roleplay (FR)

If you are being threatened, held at gunpoint, or kidnapped during roleplay, you must comply and act according to the scenario; your character's life is at risk.

Consequence: Failure to roleplay fear appropriately may result in a warning.

11. Safezones

Shooting or fighting in safezones (for example: spawn areas, police stations, fire department, and other designated safe areas) is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning; repeat or severe offences may lead to temporary bans.

12. Bribery

Attempting to bribe staff or offering bribes to avoid consequences is not allowed.

Consequence: Warning and possible disciplinary action, up to bans.

13. Border Violation

Crossing the border without proper authorization or without following the roleplay scenario is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning or temporary ban depending on severity.

14. Griefing

PURPOSELY interfering with or ruining another player's roleplay experience (for example: blocking paths, destroying roleplay props, or intentionally provoking others) is not allowed.

Consequence: Warning; repeated or severe griefing may lead to temporary or permanent bans.

15. Combat Logging

Logging out during an active roleplay scenario or while engaged in combat to avoid consequences is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning; repeated combat logging may lead to longer bans.

16. Powergaming

Forcing actions onto other players without their consent or doing things the roleplay would not reasonably allow is not permitted. Always act within the bounds of the scenario.

Consequence: Warning; repeated offences may lead to temporary bans.

17. Metagaming

Using out-of-character knowledge to influence in-game decisions or actions is prohibited. Players should only use information their character would reasonably know.

Consequence: Warning; repeated metagaming may lead to temporary bans.

18. Mass RDM

Killing multiple players without proper roleplay reason is considered mass RDM and is a severe offence.

Consequence: Immediate ban in severe cases.

19. Disruption of Roleplay

Disrupting ongoing roleplay with constant inappropriate behavior, spamming, or trolling is not allowed.

Consequence: Warning; continued disruption will result in further disciplinary action.

20. Prop Abuse

Using in-game props to block, create unfair advantages, or exploit game mechanics is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning and possible further punishment.

21. Unauthorized Use of Admin Commands

Using admin commands or tools without permission is strictly prohibited.

Consequence: Immediate ban.

22. Border Control Violations

Attempting to bypass or interfere with Border Control procedures (for example: rigging or exploiting systems) is prohibited.

Consequence: Punishment depending on severity, up to bans.

23. No Exploits or Bug Abuse

Exploiting bugs, cheats, or glitches to gain an advantage or disrupt others' gameplay is strictly prohibited.

Consequence: Immediate ban for severe exploits; warnings or temporary bans for lesser cases.

24. Respect Roleplay Boundaries

Always respect other players' comfort and boundaries. If someone asks you to stop a roleplay action, comply immediately.

Consequence: Warning; repeated violations may lead to bans.

25. Weapon Abuse

Using weapons in a way that is outside normal roleplay limits (for example: random shooting or unnecessary destruction) is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning or temporary ban depending on severity.

26. No Excessive OOC (Out-of-Character) Chat

Excessive use of OOC chat breaks immersion. Use OOC only when necessary for clarification.

Consequence: Warning; repeated abuse may lead to mute or temporary ban.

27. No Spamming

Spamming messages, commands, or actions is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning or temporary mute.

28. Respect for Roleplay Characters

Disrespecting or mocking other players' roleplay characters, or engaging in discriminatory behavior, is not allowed.

Consequence: Warning; severe cases may lead to bans.

29. No Multiple Accounts / Alt Abuse

Using multiple accounts to bypass bans, gain unfair advantage, or exploit the server is prohibited.

Consequence: Permanent ban on all accounts involved.

30. Combat Logging (duplicate)

Logging out to avoid consequences during active roleplay or combat is prohibited.

Consequence: Warning; repeated offences may lead to bans.
